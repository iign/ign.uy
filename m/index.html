Hallo

I’m a front-end designer & developer, focused on simple solutions and intuitive interfaces. more

In 2014 I founded MONTAG, a small development and design shop. Since then, I’ve been working with local and remote clients building websites, apps, brands and other stuff to help our clients be awesome at what they do.

I spend most of my days working with HTML, CSS, PHP and some JavaScript. When I’m not hacking on code I’m prototyping, wireframing or designing with Sketch, Illustrator and Photoshop.

I’m passionate about humans and solving complex problems in simple ways. I use technology and design to do so. I love photography, reading, bikes, languages and learning new stuff.
